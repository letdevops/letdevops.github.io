Privacy Policy

This page was last updated on May 24, 2018.

What This Privacy Policy Covers

This policy covers how ItPost treats personal information that ItPost collects and receives. Personal information is information about you that is personally identifiable like your name, address, email address, IP address, or phone number, and that is not otherwise publicly available. This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that ItPost does not own or control, or to people that ItPost does not employ or manage.

Information Collection and Use, General

ItPost collects personal information when you register with ItPost, and when you use ItPost products or services. When you register we ask for information such as your name and email address. Once you register with ItPost and sign in to our services, you are not anonymous to us. ItPost collects information about your transactions with us and with some of our business partners, including information about trading advice you subscribe to. ItPost automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP address, ItPost cookie information, and the page you request. ItPost uses this information to customize the content you see, fulfill your requests for products and services, improve our services, contact you, conduct research, and provide anonymous reporting for internal and external clients.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

ItPost does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people or nonaffiliated companies except to provide products or services you’ve requested, when we have your permission, or under the following circumstances:

  • We provide the information to trusted partners who work on behalf of or with ItPost under confidentiality agreements. However, these companies do not have any independent right to use or share this information.
  • We respond to subpoenas, court orders, or legal process, or to establish or exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims;
  • We believe it is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of ItPost's terms of use, or as otherwise required by law.
  • We transfer information about you if ItPost is acquired by or merged with another company. In this event, ItPost will notify you before information about you is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.
Cookies

ItPost may set and access ItPost cookies on your computer. Advertisers or other companies do not have access toItPost’s cookies.

Your Ability to Edit Your Account Information

You can edit your ItPost Account Information, including your marketing preferences, at any time. Users can opt out of receiving future marketing communications or they can unsubscribe by following instructions contained in a message they receive. We reserve the right to send you certain communications relating to the ItPost service, such as service announcements and administrative messages, that are considered part of your ItPostaccount, without offering you the opportunity to opt-out of receiving them.

Confidentiality and Security

We limit access to personal information about you to employees who we believe reasonably need to come into contact with that information to provide products or services to you or in order to do their jobs. Your ItPost Account Information is password-protected.ItPost uses industry-standard SSL-encryption to protect data transmissions.

Your Right To Be Forgotten

If you use our automated trading service, we may archive certain trading-related information, even after removing you from our main application and marketing databases, in order to comply with rules imposed on us by U.S. and international financial regulators.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

ItPost may update this policy. We will notify you about significant changes in the way we treat personal information by sending a notice to the primary email address specified in your ItPost account or by placing a prominent notice on our site.
